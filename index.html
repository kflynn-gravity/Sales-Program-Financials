Financial Scenarios: Cost vs. Cumulative Revenue

This chart compares the **total cumulative cost** of the program against the **total cumulative revenue** for three different performance models over a one-year period. The points where the revenue lines cross the cost line represent the break-even points.

**Ideal:** 3 new accounts signed per week per associate.
**Likely:** 2 new accounts signed per week per associate.
**Poor:** 1 new account signed per week per associate.

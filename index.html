Sourcing Strategy: Building the Talent Pipeline

Internal Applicants: Leverage our current pool of applicants who may be a good fit for this specific, focused role.
Staffing Agencies: Partner with agencies specializing in sales and part-time roles to quickly fill our cohort.
Post-Military Programs: Actively recruit from programs that help veterans transition into civilian careers, leveraging their discipline and teamwork.
Seasonal Industries: Target employees from seasonal businesses (e.g., pest control) who are seeking part-time work during off-seasons.
City Staffing & Services: Collaborate with local community organizations and city employment services to find talent that aligns with our core values.
Universities: Partner with local universities to recruit ambitious students looking for hands-on sales experience.

Performance & The SAE Scorecard

Sales Above Expected (SAE): Our scorecard is a clear, mission-aligned way to measure and manage performance.

About the SAE Score

The SAE Score is a metric, similar to baseball's WAR, that measures a sales representative's performance relative to the average. A score of **0** represents an average performer, a negative score indicates underperformance, and a positive score indicates overperformance.

Surcharge Sales Pilot Program

A Strategic Pilot to Staff Surcharging Sales

Presented by Keith

The Opportunity

The Problem: We've identified a clear opportunity to increase surcharging adoption but lack an efficient staffing model. Past programs have shown the difficulty of a single, one-size-fits-all approach.

The Goal: Our mission is to create a staffing model that **drives new business** while also embodying our core value of **Purpose and People Above Profits**, creating a pipeline of future Gravity leaders.

Program Vision & The Core Hypothesis

Program Overview: We will launch a pilot program with a cohort of part-time, hourly **W-2 associates** on an **Agile Pilot and Iterative Learning** basis. Their single focus will be to prospect for new surcharging appointments.

Our Core Hypothesis: We believe a single-task, focused role is the key to success. To answer which approach is best—in-person or over the phone—we propose piloting two parallel models for direct comparison.

Staffing & Scalability

Hiring Cadence: We will start with a pilot cohort of **6 associates**. This number is strategic as it gives us a proper sample size for data while maximizing efficiency relative to fixed costs.

Rapid Onboarding: Our associates will go through a streamlined **3-day onboarding process** to get them in the field or on the phone quickly.

Location Strategy: We will focus on a **single, specific tourism-heavy market** for the pilot to prove the model's viability in a controlled environment.

Staffing: Our Sourcing Strategy

Our approach is not just to hire, but to find individuals who embody the spirit of the American Dream—those who are willing to work hard for a career path.

  • Existing Applicants: We have a rich pool of past applicants who've already shown great interest in Gravity.
  • Staffing Agencies: We will partner with agencies that specialize in sales or field marketing roles.
  • Seasonal Businesses: We can tap into the talent of seasonal sales teams from pest control or landscaping companies during their off-season.
  • Universities & City Services: We will build partnerships with university career centers and local workforce development programs to find motivated talent.

Performance & The SAE Scorecard

Sales Above Expected (SAE): Our scorecard is a clear, mission-aligned way to measure and manage performance.

How We Make Decisions: We use a tiered approach to reward effort and results.

  • Low Effort: Immediate exit.
  • High Effort, Low Results: Targeted coaching and support.
  • High Performer: Eligibility for the next phase of growth.

Compensation, Costs & ROI

Compensation Model: Associates will be paid the **greater of $20/hr (for an 8-hour day) or $20 per qualified appointment set.**

Cost Projection: For a pilot of **6 associates**, the total monthly cost is approximately **$20,784**.

The Compounding ROI: The true value is in the long-term revenue stream. For every **1 associate**, we project:

  • **10 appointments per week → 3 new accounts per week.**
  • **12 new accounts per month, averaging $200 each.**
  • This generates **$2,400 in new monthly recurring revenue per associate.**

Key Risks & Mitigation

Risk Mitigation Strategy
Brand Misrepresentation A simple script, focused 3-day onboarding, and a **Quality Score** in our SAE metric to monitor and coach on professionalism.
Poor Lead Hand-off Associates will be trained to take detailed notes and a clear process will ensure full-time reps review these notes to create a seamless transition.
Legal Issues The **hourly W-2 model** and early buy-in from Legal and HR mitigate misclassification risk.
Market Saturation We will track saturation and have a plan to rotate associates or markets as needed if we scale.
Internal Pushback This program is positioned to free up full-time reps to close more deals, not to replace them.

The Program Roadmap & Off-Ramps

Timeline Goal & Benchmark Action: The Off-Ramp
1 Month Gather initial SAE data and confirm the model's viability. Pivot/Adjust: If the model fails to meet benchmarks, we stop the program.
3 Months Refine training, onboarding, and begin scaling the team. Adjust: If performance plateaus, we adjust the hiring, training, or compensation model.
6 Months Achieve consistent SAE results, prove the ROI, and identify top performers for growth. Optimize: We optimize processes, technology, and compensation to increase efficiency and revenue.
1 Year The program is a fully operational, self-sustaining pipeline. Formalize: We formalize a clear, structured **advanced sales program** for our associates and solidify the full-time career path.
